Step 1 
Take a bowl and grate chesse in it 
Step 2
Add butter in the mixing bowl and mix it for 2 to 3 mins
Step 3
Add chopped green chillies, chopped bell pepper, chopped garlic and chopped capsicum then mix it
Step 4
Take some bread slices and toast them on the fry pan firstly
Step 5 
Spread the mix on the toasted bread slices and again toast it until it is ready.
